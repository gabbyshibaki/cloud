Who are we?

We are the Saving Clouds Foundation a non-profit organization which takes and accepts pictures of our beautiful sky. We take those pictures and create large hanging tapestries for children who are stuck inside hospital rooms with no access to outside. We take these tapestries and hang them above the beds and read short stories to these children just to add some sunshine to their days.
